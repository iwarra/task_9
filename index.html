Welcome to BishBosh

This is a fun Bish-Bosh game! Enter the values for Bish, Bosh, and the loop limit to see the sequence. If a number is divisible by your Bish number, it will be replaced with 'Bish'. If it's divisible by your Bosh number, it will be replaced with 'Bosh'. If divisible by both, it will say 'Bish-Bosh'!
